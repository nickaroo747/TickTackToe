Tick Tack Toe: Coke Vs Pepsi
Play
The object of Tic Tac Toe is to get three in a row. You play on a three by three game board. The first player is Coke and the second is Pepsi. Players alternate squares on the game board until either oppent has three in a row or all nine squares are filled. Cokegoes first, and in the event that no one has three in a row, the challenge will be deamed a tie game.
